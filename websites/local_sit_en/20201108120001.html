General Information

Do you have symptoms or questions about COVID-19? Call 1 877 644-4545.

  • Communication tools
  • Consult the ABCs of going safely back to school.

Testing

For more information, consult our section testing for COVID-19.

Reminder for isolation or quarantine

There are 3 categories of people who must be in isolation or quarantine

  1. Returning from travel outside Canada (requirement under the Quarantine Act);
  2. They have been contacted by regional public health authorities to issue a notice of isolation (for confirmed cases and close case contacts);
  3. Presents symptoms consistent with COVID-19;

For the third category, you can use the self-assessment tool.
